Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MyWorld (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Android applications, including our Internet Radio Player apps and Educational Quiz apps. By using our apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Our apps may automatically collect certain non‑personal information, including:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • App usage data (screens visited, features used)
  • Anonymous analytics via Firebase Analytics (no login required)
  • Advertising ID (used by AdMob)
  • IP address and device identifiers processed by third‑party services

How We Use Information

Radio Apps

Our radio apps stream publicly available internet radio stations. We do not host radio content or track your listening history. Metadata such as station name, track title, or album art is fetched directly from the station’s public stream.

Quiz Apps

Quiz scores and progress may be stored locally on your device. Anonymous analytics may be used to improve question quality and difficulty.

Android Permissions

Our apps request only the permissions necessary to function:

  • INTERNET: Required to stream radio stations and load ads.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Allows audio playback to continue in the background.
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Used only to pause audio during incoming calls. We do not access your phone number or call logs.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (if used): For playback controls and important app alerts.

Third‑Party Services & Advertising

We use third‑party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics. These services may collect information to provide analytics and personalized content. No login is required to use our apps, and all analytics data collection is performed anonymously.

Firebase Analytics

Firebase Analytics is integrated into all our apps to collect anonymous usage data. No user login is required. Firebase collects:

  • Anonymous user identifiers
  • App session duration and frequency
  • Feature usage and user engagement metrics
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Device and app environment data

This data is used solely for improving app performance, understanding user behavior, and enhancing features. All data is transmitted and stored anonymously without linking to any personal identity.

AdMob (Google Mobile Ads)

AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location
  • Device and app interaction data
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

This information is used for ad personalization (where allowed), fraud prevention, and analytics. In regions where required, we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request consent.

You can review Google’s policies here:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Any data collected by third‑party services such as AdMob or Firebase is retained according to their respective privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
