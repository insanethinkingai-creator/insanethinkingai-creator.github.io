Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MyWorld ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our Android applications, including our Internet Radio Players and Educational Quiz applications. By using our apps, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

We believe in data minimization. We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal identification (such as names or email addresses) to use the core features of our applications.

  • Radio Apps: These apps function as players for publicly available internet radio streams. We do not host content, and we do not monitor your specific listening habits.
  • Quiz Apps: We may store quiz scores and progress locally on your device. If analytics are enabled, we collect anonymized data regarding quiz difficulty and completion rates to improve our educational content.

Android Permissions

Our apps request only the permissions necessary to function:

  • INTERNET: Required to access radio streams and fetch quiz updates.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required for Radio apps to maintain audio playback while you use other apps or when the screen is locked.
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Used solely to detect incoming calls so the radio player can automatically pause and resume for your convenience.

Third-Party Services & Advertising

We may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you or provide advertisements. We use AdMob to serve ads, which helps keep our apps free. You can find the privacy policies of our providers here:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics

Children’s Privacy

Our educational quiz apps are intended for a general audience, including students. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent and aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
